Our Story

More Than a Cut —
It's a Craft

At Fades & Blades, we believe every visit should feel like an experience. From the moment you sit in the chair, you're in the hands of skilled barbers who take pride in every line, every fade, and every finish.

We've been serving the Ranelagh community since 2018, building a reputation for precision cuts, a welcoming atmosphere, and a no-rush approach to grooming. Whether it's your first time or your fiftieth, you'll leave looking sharp.
